Eat right, gain might: Your personalized path to a stronger, healthier you

Diet Plan 1

Weight Gain Diet Plan

Monday

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with cheese and spinach, Whole-grain toast with avocado
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Greek yogurt with granola and sliced bananas
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken or tofu wrap with vegetables and hummus, Mixed fruit salad
  • Afternoon Snack: Protein smoothie with whole milk, berries, and protein powder, Handful of cashews or almonds
  • Dinner: Baked salmon or lentil stew, Quinoa pilaf, Steamed broccoli with cheese sauce
  • Evening Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple slices, Whole-grain crackers with cheese

Tuesday

  • Breakfast: Overnight oats with whole milk, chia seeds, and mixed nuts, Banana or apple
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Protein bar or nut bar
  • Lunch: Beef or turkey burger with whole-grain bun, Sweet potato fries, Mixed greens salad with olive oil dressing
  • Afternoon Snack: Peanut butter sandwich on whole-grain bread, Carrot sticks with hummus
  • Dinner: Grilled chicken thighs or tempeh with brown rice, Stir-fried vegetables in sesame oil
  • Evening Snack: Protein shake with almond milk, banana, and a scoop of protein powder

Wednesday

  • Breakfast: Whole-grain pancakes with maple syrup and mixed berries, Greek yogurt with honey
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Apple slices with almond butter
  • Lunch: Tuna or chickpea salad wrap, Quinoa salad with veggies
  • Afternoon Snack: Protein smoothie with whole milk, mango, and protein powder, Trail mix with dried fruits and nuts
  • Dinner: Beef stir-fry with noodles or rice, Steamed edamame or green beans
  • Evening Snack: Cottage cheese with peaches or berries

Thursday to Saturday

You can mix and match meals from the previous days to ensure variety and meet your calorie and nutrient needs. Focus on including a balance of protein, carbohydrates, healthy fats, and plenty of fruits and vegetables throughout these days. Adjust portion sizes based on your individual requirements and activity level.

Diet Plan 2

Weight Loss Diet Plan

Monday

  • Breakfast: Scrambled egg whites with spinach and tomatoes, Whole-grain toast or oatmeal
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Greek yogurt with berries
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cucumbers, and a vinaigrette dressing
  • Afternoon Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus
  • Dinner: Baked fish with lemon and herbs, Steamed vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower, green beans)
  • Evening Snack: Air-popped popcorn or a small handful of almonds

Tuesday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, almond milk, and protein powder
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Cottage cheese with sliced cucumbers or bell peppers
  • Lunch: Turkey or tofu wrap with whole-grain tortilla and lettuce, tomato, and mustard
  • Afternoon Snack: Edamame (steamed soybeans)
  • Dinner: Grilled shrimp skewers with zucchini and bell peppers, Quinoa or brown rice
  • Evening Snack: Low-fat string cheese or a small apple

Wednesday

  • Breakfast: Chia seed pudding with almond milk and berries, Whole-grain toast with avocado
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Rice cakes with almond butter
  • Lunch: Grilled vegetable salad with quinoa and a balsamic vinaigrette
  • Afternoon Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and walnuts
  • Dinner: Baked tofu with stir-fried vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, mushrooms) in a light soy sauce
  • Evening Snack: Sliced cucumbers with guacamole

Thursday to Saturday

You can mix and match meals from the previous days to ensure variety and meet your calorie and nutrient needs. Focus on including a balance of protein, carbohydrates, healthy fats, and plenty of fruits and vegetables throughout these days. Adjust portion sizes based on your individual requirements and activity level.

Muscle-Building Diet Plan
